Our Story

Hey there! We’re SaiNiharika,Kundana and Sravanivarma best friends with a shared love for sweets. One day, as we munched on pastries at our favorite café, SaiNiharika declared, “Let’s start our own bakery! We can make the most amazing treats ever.” With our heads full of sugar and excitement, we decided to name it “SS HomeBakes.” We transformed SaiNiharika’s tiny kitchen into our baking haven, where the oven often turned our cakes into quirky shapes. Our first day was a bit of a flop—Muffin, Sravani’s cat, was our only customer! But we didn’t give up. We gave out free samples, and soon enough, a local food blogger loved our creations. The buzz was incredible! Despite our baking blunders—like the infamous “Salty Surprise” cake—we made a name for ourselves with our unique, fun approach to baking. At SS HomeBakes, we’re passionate about using only the finest ingredients. Each treat is crafted with care, from rich chocolates to fresh, locally sourced fruits. Our commitment to quality shines through in every bite, and we take pride in creating homemade goodness that makes people smile. Our motto? “If at first you don’t succeed, bake, bake again!” We’ve turned our baking mishaps into delicious successes and love sharing our journey with you. Thanks for being part of our sweet adventure!
